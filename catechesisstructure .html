Structure

Our program will follow the structure of a catechetical program created by Metropolitan Kalistos Ware called "Path of Salvation"
The "Path of Salvation" project was designed and carried out by a group of specialists from the Institute for Christian-Orthodox Studies
at the University of Cambridge in Great Britain The proposed topicsto be supported are the following:

  1. The search for faith
  2. God - Holy Trinity: "The Lover of People"
  3. To be human, fully alive. Man as the fulfillment of life
  4. Why did Jesus come to us?
  5. Salvation in Christ.
  6. The Holy Spirit.
  1. What is the church? Isn't God enough?
  2. Living faith. I. Divine Liturgy, a pearl of great price.
  3. Living the faith. II. Holy Mysteries.
  4. Living the faith. III. Holy Scripture, prayer and fasting.
  5. Christian behavior
  6. Heaven on earth, members of the Church and citizens of heaven.
