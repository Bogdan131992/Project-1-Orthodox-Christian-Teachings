Welcome to our website!

We organize, for the Orthodox Christian community in Leipzig, free sessions of twelve catechetical meetings for three different age groups. The sessions are for those who want to improve their knowledge about their faith.

What is faith and why is it important?

Faith is a gift from God, this great gift must be increased through a personal relationship with Him

Seeing that many believers, even though they are baptized, no longer know their own faith, but they have the desire to find more about it, we decided to help them by organizing orthodox catechesis. In these catechesis we will discuss the fundamental themes of the Orthodox Christian faith.

You can find the list of the twelve themes in the menu bar at the top by accessing the structure page.

Our meetings take place in the Orthodox Church in Leipzig, Germany

  • Every Thursday from 6:00 p.m.

  • Adress: Russenstr. 194, 04288 Leipzig.

  • We start with a small prayer before every meeting
  • We will have twelve meetings in groups of 20-30 people, for three different age groups.
  • In each meeting, the priest or catechists will present a theme for about an hour
  • Followed by half an hour of dialogue with questions and answers
  • In the end will enjoy a small agape
  • Priority geven by order of registration!
  • Don't forget to bring a pen, notebook and a Bible!
