"So faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the word of God"

(Romans 10,17)

Fill out the form and reserve a place at our spiritual meetings!

Meetings are held at the Orthodox Church in Leipzig, Germany,every Thursday from 6:00 p.m.

  • Our program consists of twelve meetings in groups of 20 people, in different age groups.
  • In each meeting, the priest or catechists will present a theme for about an hour
    • then there will be another half hour of dialogue with the believers followed by a small agape!
  • Don't forget to bring a pen, notebook and a Bible!
  • Priority is in order of registration!
